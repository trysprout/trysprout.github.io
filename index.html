Hiring is one of the most crucial components to the success of your company. It is also one of the trickiest. Fortunately, Sprout can help alleviate some of the burden by providing valuable feedback on a candidate’s technical abilities.

  • How it works

    Sprout evaluates a candidate’s technical ability by:

    1) Analyzing the candidate’s resume using our proprietary algorithms.

    2) Sending the candidate a 5-minute quiz that is custom-generated based on the contents of the candidate’s resume. Using this hybrid approach, we are able to build a profile of the candidate’s technical abilities and generate a summary, a numerical score, and a proceed/don’t proceed recommendation.
